Coanda

Coanda takes the refined structure of Lock Sans and introduces playful stencil cutouts, offering a bold and industrial edge. This variant strikes a unique balance between functionality and creativity, making it perfect for standout designs.


Available in six weights from ExtraLight to Black, Coanda features glyph alternates for key characters like ‘a,’ ‘g,’ ’t,’ and ‘Q,’ along with arrows, circled numbers, as well as alternate commas and quotes. Coanda can be used interchangeably with Lock Sans, with no impact on text flow. The character set covers most Latin-based languages.


The stencil details give it a utilitarian, yet crafted feel, making it a bold choice for headlines, posters, or branding work where a more industrial and playful look is required.


Coanda belongs to the Lock family – a collection designed by Mark Bloom and Diana Ovezea. The family explores the intersection of broad-nib calligraphy and industrial design, offering both Sans and Serif variants with matching stencil counterparts.
